Logistics & Need-to-Knows

Reference section — practical details for the trip

Lodging

  • • Jun 14–16: Turnberry, a Luxury Collection Resort (Ayrshire)
  • • Jun 16–21: Greyfriars Hotel, 129 North Street, St. Andrews
  • • Jun 16–21: Lorimer House, 19 Murray Park, St. Andrews (self check-in code: 8591)
  • • Jun 16–21: Nethan Guest House, 17 Murray Park, St. Andrews
  • • All accommodation prepaid · breakfast included at all properties
  • • Lorimer House check-in: 3pm · check-out: 10am

Money & Cards

  • • Currency: British Pound Sterling (£)
  • • Secure some Sterling before you go — caddies, tips, and small purchases
  • • Use a credit card with no international transaction fees
  • • Old Course green fee to be paid directly on day of play (not covered in package)
  • • Caddie tip: minimum 20% of caddie fee, paid in cash

Phones & Comms

  • • Call your carrier — activate an international plan before departure
  • • Google Photos folder: share the first airport photo

Weather & Dress Code

  • • Scotland in June: cool, breezy, and unpredictable — pack layers
  • • Most important skill: putting rain gear on and off fast
  • • Smart casual required on courses and in clubhouses
  • • Collared shirts, tailored shorts only — no denim, tracksuits, or sleeveless tees
  • • No headwear permitted inside clubhouses
  • • UK power outlets: 240V, three-prong — bring plug adapters

Old Course Ballot

  • • Daily lottery operated by St. Andrews Links Trust
  • • Draw happens the evening before the desired round
  • • If drawn: walk to/from Old Course (no transport needed)
  • • Green fee paid directly on day — not included in package
  • • Even without a tee time: walk it, cross the Swilcan Bridge, peer into Hell Bunker

Dining

  • • Dining recommendations in the Hidden Links client portal
  • • Book dinner reservations as soon as possible — popular spots fill early
  • • Accommodation providers can also suggest local options
  • • Arrive at each course at least 40 minutes before tee time

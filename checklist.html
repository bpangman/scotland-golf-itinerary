Pre-Trip Checklist

Everything to handle before June 14th

Don't Forget Before You Go

Passport valid — must not expire within 6 months of return date
UK ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization) completed — apply at gov.uk before you travel
ETA & United app: scan passport, upload ETA to United app
International phone plan activated with your carrier
Credit card confirmed with no international transaction fees
British Pound Sterling secured for caddies, tips, and small purchases
Rain gear packed (jacket + pants — quick on/off is key)
Golf clubs — shipped ahead or packed for check-in
UK plug adapter (240V, 3-prong) packed
Read the full Travel Plans PDF — check the hot links inside
Enter Old Course ballot via St. Andrews Links Trust portal
Google Photos: confirm access to shared folder
Book dinner reservations ASAP via Hidden Links portal recommendations
